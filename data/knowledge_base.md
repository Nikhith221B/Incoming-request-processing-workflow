# Mini Knowledge Base

## Refunds
Refund reviews are normally completed within 3 to 5 business days after all required transaction details are available.

## Billing disputes
Duplicate charge disputes should be escalated to Billing Support with transaction date, amount, and account identifier.

## Account updates
Account activation and profile changes are handled by Account Operations with a standard 1-business-day SLA.

## Response times
General enquiries are normally answered within 1 business day. Complaints and escalations receive priority handling.

## Escalations
Critical incidents, legal threats, safety issues, outages, or supervisor requests should be routed to a supervisor and paused for human review.
